Conditions

Target

Distance is required to determine how far out to calculate ballistics data
Chart stepping is required to determine how many rows to calculate
Slant degrees is required to determine vertical hold over or angle scope adjustments needed. Both up and down slant angles result in the need to aim low.
Target speed is required to determine horizontal lead hold or scope adjustments needed.

Weather

Altitude is required to determine atmospheric density
Temperature is required to determine atmospheric density
Barometeric Pressure is required to determine atmospheric density
Relative Humidity is required to determine atmospheric density
Wind Velocity is required to calculate bullet drift
Wind Angle is required to determine the winds vector impact on bullet drift. Allowed values are 0-90 degrees.
Target and weather information data are not preserved between sessions and will revert back to their defaults
